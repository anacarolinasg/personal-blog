Who are you?

No, I’m not asking about the person others see on the outside. I’m not asking about the one you fake to be when you want to be seeing in a specific way.

I’m asking who you are when no one’s watching. I’m asking about the things you do unpretentiously. The good you do without think about any return. Think… how good would you be if you could never take any self benefit from it?

And who you are when you lose? And when you win? Who are you without all the makeup and fancy clothes? Who are you when there’s nothing left but your own conscience? And more than that… who are you in comparison with the one you wished to be? And who’s that? Is that you?
